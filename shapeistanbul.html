Shape Istanbul

Services

Brand Identity, User Interface Design, Marketing & Social Media Consulting

Overview

We tried to reflect the streets to our works in Shape Istanbul which is inspired by many branches of art to produce specially designed products. We have designed an interface to highlight the videos and the products that we processed from different regions of Istanbul.

We processed videos not exceeding one minute and aimed to reflect all of the products for the brand which has Intagram focused sales. Also we created brutalist style composition for the social media accounts of Shape.

While working with this brand which tries to reflect language and the differences of the street to its products, we specially gave importance to applicability. In particular, we have ensured that the corporate identity is simple to express with spray and sticker. And again, to reflect this texture, we used stone walls, rusty surfaces and old settlements as background.
