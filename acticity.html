Services

Mobile Application Design, Development, Copywriting, Illustration, Animation, Business Development

Overview

In Acticity, a Studio WTF project, we produced the MVP, after a process of development of the idea. In preparation process of this prototype, we created the detail pages and project flow while creating the interface and user experience to guide us in designing. After designing the interface of Acticity, we started to develop the algorithm that analyzes the Spotify preferences to be used in user matches and we have determined the personas which will be used by algorithm. At the same time, we have prepared marketing analyzes, marketing plans and promotional animations to be used in the marketing process, as well as partnership agreements with companies such as Biletix.

Our first step in Acticity was to create a logo and corporate identitiy for the young audiance we aimed. We decided to prepare an orange colored logo which has hand-writing style as a result of our negotiations in our team about color choices and style which we used as a basis in every work of Acticity.

We have managed to create a flow to help users in every step of event process from concert selection to ticket purchase with this. For instance, we worked on a ticketing method that users can use their mobile phones instead of showing the tickes which we planned to reduce the paper consumption.
