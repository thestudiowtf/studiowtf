MedTeacher

Online lecture platform for medicine students
Services

Web Application Design, Development, User Experience Strategy Design

Overview

In Medteacher which is an online lecture platform built for medical students, we put a particular effort to user experience of the platform in order to simplify the schedule of the students taking lots of lectures at the same time. We've taken the simplicity and productivity as our two focusing points while designing the user experience and interface. We also focused to mobile application integration which is likely to be added in the next progress of the project.

In this project, which was created for medical students following lots of courses at the same time, we created a dashboard that shows all the data for students to follow up on which course video they were staying in. As well as we showed them all the data that would help the student in their specially designed dashboard.
