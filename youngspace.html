Youngspace

A network for youngpreneurs
Services

User Interface Design, Illustration, Copywriting, Business Development

Overview

We base our target audience on the Z generation on Youngspace, a network platform for young entrepreneurs, and we have progressed through this theme in the texts and illustrations we have prepared.

During the business development phaprocessse of the project, we defined the users on the platform under three different titles: Director, Developer, Designer. In this way, we tried to make the activities planned to be done and the features to be created for different user types more effective.
