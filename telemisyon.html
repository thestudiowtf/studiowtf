Telemisyon

An online platform for movies & TV series
Services

Full Stack Development, Web Application Design, User Experience Strategy Design

Overview

At Telemisyon project, we have promoted the interface and design language to a completely different level in order to stand out from the other watching platforms. We also searched ways to provide the most appropriate content for different users by using a special filtering algorithm.

We redesigned the watching experience by using a different coloring system for users who are following multiple series at the same time. In addition, with a special search algorithm, we have developed a system that allows users to search for contents with content-specialized keywords.

Instead of just including player on the series and movie details pages, we have enabled users to access more detailed information about that content and have designed an interactive rating experience.
